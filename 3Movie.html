Dhruva

  • Release Year: 2016
  • Director: Surender Reddy
  • Main Actors: Ram Charan, Arvind Swamy, Rakul Preet Singh
  • Genre: Action, Thriller

Synopsis

Dhruva, an IPS officer, aims to take down Siddharth Abhimanyu, a brilliant but corrupt scientist.

Review

  • Story: Engaging and intelligent, focusing on a cat-and-mouse game.
  • Performance: Ram Charan is intense, and Arvind Swamy excels as the antagonist.
  • Direction: Surender Reddy's direction is sharp, with excellent cinematography.
  • Music: Hiphop Tamizha's score complements the film's intense atmosphere.

Rating

Rating: ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️☆ (4/5)

Recommendation

Highly recommended for fans of action-packed thrillers.
